Methodology & trust

What NutriForge is

A curated, evidence-graded food reference for clinical and public use, with emphasis on African ingredients and diaspora plates. It is not a full national food composition table: many trace nutrients are described narratively (micronutrients field) rather than as a complete analytic panel.

Data & updates

  • Macros and core composition align with USDA FoodData Central, WHO references, and peer-reviewed sources cited in card copy.
  • Traditional dishes use typical-portion estimates; recipes vary — treat numbers as orientation, not laboratory analysis.
  • Editorial updates are batched; check the footer version string for the build you are viewing.

Drug–food screening

The drawer matches your medication search against keyword rules and each food’s limits text. Severities are editorial flags for teaching, not verified drug–nutrient interaction pairs. It does not replace Lexicomp, Stockley’s, your formulary, or pharmacist review. Always confirm with the prescriber.

AI assistant

The assistant uses an external model with a short system prompt and a slice of your current filter context. On hosted builds, requests are sent to this site’s /api/chat proxy so your browser never holds an API key. It can still be wrong — verify critical facts on the food cards and original literature.

Visceral adiposity (VAT) protocol

For clinicians and motivated clients working on visceral fat, metabolic markers, and structured meal timing: we host an evidence-aligned protocol hub with an in-app outline (always available here) and optional gated PDF / web outline from your deployment.

Privacy & portability

Diary, goals, and client profiles are stored only in this browser (localStorage). Clearing site data removes them. There is no signed-in account in this build.

Use More → Download data backup to save a JSON file (keep it private — it may contain visit notes). Use Restore from backup on this or another device to reload that snapshot.

Daily protocol summary

Fixed fasting/eating window with timed hydration, fibre, protein-forward meals, and sleep-support habits. Exact clock times and kcal splits live in the secured PDF.

General food base

Tiered list by mechanisms (protein leverage, fibre, thermogenic cues, fat quality, volume). Each item carries an evidence grade — interpret alongside your clinician and labs.

African food base

Parallel list emphasising culturally familiar staples and condiments; lower funded trial tiers are flagged explicitly while mechanisms may still align with established pathways.

Daily timelines

Separate schedules for general and African pantry emphasis; spacing for iron absorption vs tea/polyphenols is spelled out in the document.

Protocol rules

Non‑negotiables include protein-first sequencing, starch cooling for resistant starch, carb-cycle days, fluid targets with psyllium, and cooking-fat discipline.

Biomarker targets

Triglyceride/HDL ratio flagged as priority alongside fasting insulin, HOMA‑IR, waist, hsCRP, and related markers — always contextualise with testing frequency your team recommends.

Synergies & meal rotation

Pairing logic and a one-week rotation template to reduce decision fatigue while keeping fibre and protein thresholds.

Periodisation & training

Three phases with diet breaks and guidance to sync intake around harder training days.

Supplements, hydration, lifestyle

Optional stacks, fluid rules, stress/cortisol hygiene — verify interactions with prescriptions.

Shopping, portions, eating out

Lists, cost thinking, portion visuals, and restaurant tactics.

Safety & contraindications

Read this in the PDF before starting. Pregnancy, diabetes medications/insulin, anticoagulants, renal disease, history of eating disorders, and acute illness change whether phased deficits, fasting, or supplement suggestions are appropriate.
